The Monolith Apartment

RESIDENTIAL • LONDON • 2024

VISION & IDENTITY

LOCATION MAYFAIR, LONDON
YEAR 2024
AREA 3,200 SQ FT

"A study in reduction, where the presence of shadow is as vital as the movement of light across raw stone."

The Monolith Apartment was conceived as an urban sanctuary that rejects the noise of the city in favor of architectural silence. Our approach focused on a limited palette of high-integrity materials: bush-hammered travertine, smoked oak, and hand-applied lime plaster.

By removing unnecessary partitions, we allowed the natural rhythm of the building's structure to dictate the flow of the home. The central 'Monolith'—a sculptural kitchen island carved from a single block of Pietra Gray marble—serves as the anchor for the entire residence, bridging the transition between social and private zones.

MINIMALISM MATERIALITY BESPOKE

Specifications & Materiality
